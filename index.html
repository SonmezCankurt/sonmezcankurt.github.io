About Me

As a game developer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering, I am passionate about creating engaging and immersive games for various platforms. With a strong foundation in C# and object-oriented programming (OOP), I adhere to SOLID principles to ensure clean and maintainable code.

I worked as a game developer at Jenixa Games for a year, where I contributed to the design, development, and testing of multiple mobile games using C# and Unity for Android. Two of these games are currently published and have received positive feedback from users and reviewers. I also earned two certifications from Unity, Creative Core and Junior Programmer, demonstrating my proficiency and skills in using C# and Unity.

My current goal is to develop casual and mid-core games with more complex systems and modular structures, using my software development experience and game development skills. I am always eager to learn new technologies and tools, and to collaborate with other creative and talented developers. You can check out my portfolio at sonmezcankurt.github.io.
